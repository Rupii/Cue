# Seedance AI Promo Video Prompt Guide (Human Interaction + Outdoor)

Below is the detailed prompt list for creating an engaging promotional video for the "Cue" app. This version shows a person physically holding and interacting with the app in a bright, modern, outdoor "lifestyle" setting to make it feel vibrant and relatable.

### Overall Style Settings (Use these for all clips)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (Vertical) for TikTok/Reels/Shorts, or 16:9 for YouTube.
- **Style/Vibe:** Bright, sunny, outdoor lifestyle, shallow depth of field, photorealistic, cinematic lifestyle B-roll, natural sunlight, over-the-shoulder and first-person POVs.
- **Colors:** Vibrant natural tones (greens, blues, warm sunlight) contrasting beautifully with the sleek app interfaces.

### Editing Advice:
1. Generate these clips using Seedance. Adding humans *can* sometimes confuse image-to-video AI with fingers (generating 6 fingers, etc.). If a hand looks slightly weird, regenerate that specific shot or cut away quickly in the final edit!
2. If Seedance distorts the text in the screenshots too much, open a video editor (like CapCut), place the Seedance-generated video on the bottom layer, and place the *original, clean screenshot* directly over the top of the phone screen, tracking its motion if possible.
3. Add an upbeat, positive lifestyle acoustic or light electronic track.
